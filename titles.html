Club achievements

The titles of Vale de Ferro

Throughout its history, Vale de Ferro built a journey marked by overcoming challenges, consistency, and great campaigns. These are the trophies that established the club as a powerhouse.

Achievement summary

Vale de Ferro has national, international, and regional titles, with emphasis on the three-time Brazilian championship and the historic first Libertadores title, which changed the club’s level forever.

National Titles

  • Brazilian Championship: 3 titles — 2018, 2021 and 2024
  • Brazil Cup: 2 titles — 2017 and 2022

International Titles

  • Libertadores: 1 title — 2023
  • South American Recopa: 1 title — 2024

Regional Titles

  • State Championship: 8 titles

Historic mark

Each achievement represents an important phase in the club’s evolution: from regional strength to consolidation as a football giant. Vale de Ferro supporters recognize in these trophies the result of hard work, unity, and identity.
