Why I chose Vale de Ferro

A personal view about the club and the reasons that led me to use it as the basis for this project.

My Opinion

Vale de Ferro is a team that represents much more than just football. Even though it is a fictional club, it was built with a strong identity, based on values such as discipline, hard work, and overcoming challenges. These elements make the team interesting and different from many others.

The choice of this club for the creation of this website and this project was mainly due to the richness of its history. The idea of a team that emerged from an industrial environment, with roots tied to the effort of workers, brings a unique and very striking concept.

In addition, Vale de Ferro has a consistent growth path, winning important titles and becoming a powerhouse in the national and international scene. This makes it possible to explore several aspects, such as evolution, achievements, and sports identity.

Another important point is the club's visual style. The black and red colors convey strength and intensity, combining perfectly with the team's proposal and contributing to a modern and impactful look on the website.

For these reasons, I consider Vale de Ferro an excellent choice for this project, as it allows both the technical and creative sides to be developed, resulting in complete, interesting, and well-structured content.
